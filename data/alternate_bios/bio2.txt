Renan has a PhD, MS, and BS in Computer Science from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro. He spent a year at Missouri State University, did a summer internship at Stanford University, and was a visiting researcher at Inria, France, always working with data science and big data engineering problems. Since 2015, he has worked at IBM Research, playing essential roles on dozens of projects with worldwide researchers, engineers, and clients in different industries, including energy and finance. He works with data integration, cloud computing, parallel and distributed data processing, knowledge graphs, data governance, and data lineage. He applies his expertise to improve the workflows of AI practitioners using cloud platforms, enabling them to build models with higher quality faster. He has supported various AI workflows, including image classification and NLP. Regarding technologies, he is skilled with Python, Java, Shell scripting, C++; Spark, Hadoop; Kafka, Elasticsearch, RabbitMQ, Redis; Airflow, Kubeflow; Postgres, MySQL, MS SQL Server; MongoDB, AllegroGraph, Jena, Virtuoso; Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift, Travis, Jenkins.
